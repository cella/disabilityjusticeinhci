Call for Submissions

We are interested in a variety of submissions that concern research, design, refections, and even personal experiences related to disability justice and HCI. We are paying special attention to the following topics:

  • Intersections of disability with race, gender, sexuality, and class
  • Accessible ways of doing research which also reciprocate community needs
  • Disability justice and AI/ML systems
  • Disability justice in the Global South
  • Tensions in academia and disability justice

We invite researchers, designers, practitioners, activists, and community members to submit materials that reflect their interest in the aforementioned topics. These materials may be one of the following:

  • 300-word abstract
  • 1-2 page statement of interest or position paper
  • Short video (45 seconds or more)
  • Blog post
  • Pictorial

All submissions must be accessible. Please refer to our resources section with detailed instructions on how to make various submissions accessible.
